Bold and Strong

To make our text bold we use B tag.

To make our text strong we use strong tag

Different Between "Bold" and "Strong" Tag.

Bold Tag makes the text thicker,(for styling) and Strong Tag makes the text thicker as well as tells the browser that text inside strong tag is improtant.

Italic and Emphasis

To make our text Italic in html, we use i tag.

To make our text Emphasis in html, we use em tag.

Difference Between "Italic" and "Emphasis"

Italic Tag for styling by making text italic whereasEmphasis Tag makes the word italic as well as also emphasizes the word, giving it more importance
